Got questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Everything you need to know before starting your transformation journey.

How quickly can I expect to see results?
+

Most clients notice visible changes within 3-4 weeks of consistent training and following the nutrition plan. Significant transformations typically occur within 12-16 weeks. Remember, sustainable results take time — we're building habits for life, not quick fixes.

Do I need gym access for online coaching?
+

Not necessarily. I design programs based on your available equipment. Whether you have a full gym, basic home setup, or just bodyweight, I'll create an effective plan. We discuss your situation during the initial consultation.

What makes your nutrition plans different?
+

My plans are built around foods you actually enjoy. No extreme restrictions, no boring chicken-and-rice monotony. I teach you flexible dieting principles so you can eat out, travel, and live normally while hitting your goals. Sustainability is key.

Can I switch between plans if my needs change?
+

Absolutely. Life changes — work schedules, travel, injuries. You can upgrade, downgrade, or modify your plan at any time with 7 days notice. I believe in adapting to your life, not forcing your life to adapt to a rigid program.

Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied?
+

Yes. I offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all new clients. If you don't feel the program is right for you after two weeks, I'll refund your first month no questions asked. I'm confident in my methods, but I want you to feel secure in your investment.
